GLOBAL AMERICAN WHEEL

How it plays

  • Everyone plays the same global spin and sees the same outcome.
  • Bet during the countdown. Bets lock when the wheel starts spinning.
  • American roulette has 0 and 00. Outside bets lose on either green pocket.
  • Straights pay 35:1, splits 17:1, streets 11:1, corners 8:1, six-lines 5:1, top line 6:1, dozens and columns 2:1, outside even-money bets 1:1.

Quick covers

  • Neighbors places five straight bets: the chosen pocket plus two wheel neighbors on each side.
  • Finals places straight bets on every table number ending in the chosen digit.
  • Undo, Clear, Rebet, and Double only work while betting is open.
