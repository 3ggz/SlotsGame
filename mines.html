HOW TO PLAY

  • Pick how many mines (3–47) to hide on the vault.
  • Hit START, then reveal tiles. Each safe tile is a diamond and grows your multiplier.
  • Reveal a mine and the round ends — that bet is lost.
  • Cash out any time after the first safe reveal to bank bet × multiplier.
  • Clear every safe tile and the full vault pays out.

BOARD SIZE

  • The vault grows with the mines: 3–15 mines on a 5×5, 16–27 on a 6×6, 28–47 on a 7×7.
  • More mines means tighter odds but much bigger jumps per safe reveal.

MULTIPLIER

  • Math is fair with a 1% house edge.
  • The NEXT pill always shows what your next safe reveal pays.
