If your phone says "can't reach", Windows Firewall is blocking the connection. Run this in PowerShell as Administrator (one time):

New-NetFirewallRule -DisplayName "Slots Server" -Direction Inbound -Protocol TCP -LocalPort 8080 -Action Allow
