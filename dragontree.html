HOW TO PLAY

  • Set your bet, then tap STRIKE THE TREE
  • Every strike has a 25% chance to trigger the Golden Shower Bonus
  • When bonus triggers, leaves rain down and 3–5 reveal prize multipliers
  • Multipliers are summed, then paid out as total × your bet

PRIZE TIERS

  • Common — Lucky Penny, Dragon's Tip (0.5× – 1×)
  • Uncommon — Temple Offering, Jade Blessing (2× – 5×)
  • Rare — Dragon's Breath, Golden Scale (8× – 10×)
  • Epic — Fortune Awakened (20×)
  • Legendary — Dragon's Hoard (50×)
  • Mythic — Jade Emperor's Gift (100×)

EXPECTED RETURN

Drop weights are tuned so an average bonus round pays around 6–10× your bet. Mythic prizes are extremely rare — see the paytable for full odds.
